Our Mission

Operating as a ministry under Church of God Mission Int’l Inc. (CGMI), We are committed to the Christian Mission of promoting the full development of the whole child in Christ: Spiritually, Mentally, Physically, Socially.


Our Values

Word of faith Group of Schools Abuja prepares students from across Nigeria for success as ethical leaders in today’s global society. Students at Word of Faith Schools Abuja represent a vast range of ethnicities and traditions. This fosters the cosmopolitan environment you expect of a world-class Christian School, reflecting the remarkable diversity of the Abuja City itself.
